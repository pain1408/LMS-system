Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the CUET Library Management System

Search FAQ

How do I register for the library system?

To register for the CUET Library Management System:

  1. Click on the "Register" button on the homepage
  2. Fill in your Student ID, full name, and email address
  3. Select your department and academic session
  4. Create a secure password
  5. Submit the form and wait for account verification

Your account will be activated within 24 hours after verification by the library staff.

I forgot my password. How can I reset it?

To reset your password:

  1. Go to the login page
  2. Click on "Forgot Password?"
  3. Enter your Student ID or email address
  4. Check your email for reset instructions
  5. Follow the link to create a new password

If you don't receive the email within 10 minutes, check your spam folder or contact the library staff.

How many books can I borrow at once?

Students can borrow up to 5 books at any given time. This limit includes:

  • Regular textbooks and reference materials
  • Research journals and publications
  • Digital resources (if applicable)

Once you return a book, you can immediately borrow another one, provided you don't have any outstanding fines.

How long can I keep a borrowed book?

The standard borrowing period is 30 days from the date of borrowing. You can:

  • Renew books up to 2 times (total 90 days maximum)
  • Renew online through your dashboard
  • Renew only if no other student has pre-booked the book

Reference books and high-demand textbooks may have shorter borrowing periods.

How does the pre-booking system work?

The pre-booking system allows you to reserve books before visiting the library:

  1. Search for the book you want
  2. Click "Pre-book" if available
  3. The book will be reserved for 24 hours
  4. Visit the library within this time to collect it
  5. If not collected, the reservation expires automatically

You can pre-book up to 5 books simultaneously.

How are library fines calculated?

Library fines are calculated as follows:

  • Overdue books: ৳5 per day per book
  • Lost books: Full replacement cost + ৳100 processing fee
  • Damaged books: Repair cost or replacement cost

Fines start accumulating from the day after the due date. Weekend days and holidays are included in the calculation.

How can I pay my library fines online?

You can pay fines online using mobile banking:

  1. Log into your student dashboard
  2. Go to "Pay Fines" section
  3. Select your payment method (bKash, Nagad, Rocket)
  4. Enter the payment amount
  5. Complete the transaction using your mobile banking app
  6. Upload the transaction screenshot for verification

Payments are usually processed within 2-4 hours during working days.

How can I upload study notes?

To upload study notes:

  1. Go to the "Notes" section in your dashboard
  2. Click "Upload New Note"
  3. Fill in the title and description
  4. Select the related book/subject
  5. Upload your PDF file (max 10MB)
  6. Submit for admin review

Notes will be available for download after admin approval, usually within 2-3 working days.

What file formats are accepted for notes?

Currently, we only accept PDF files for study notes with the following requirements:

  • Maximum file size: 10MB
  • Clear, readable content
  • No copyrighted material without permission
  • Original work or properly cited sources

Files in other formats (Word, PowerPoint, images) should be converted to PDF before uploading.

The website is not loading properly. What should I do?

If you're experiencing technical issues:

  1. Clear your browser cache and cookies
  2. Try using a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari)
  3. Check your internet connection
  4. Disable browser extensions temporarily
  5. Try accessing from a different device

If the problem persists, contact our technical support team at support@cuet.ac.bd

Can I access the system from my mobile phone?

Yes! The CUET Library Management System is fully responsive and works on:

  • Smartphones (Android, iOS)
  • Tablets and iPads
  • Desktop computers
  • Laptops

All features are available on mobile devices, including book search, pre-booking, fine payment, and note downloads.

What are the library operating hours?

CUET Library operating hours:

  • Sunday to Thursday: 8:00 AM - 8:00 PM
  • Friday: 8:00 AM - 12:00 PM, 2:00 PM - 6:00 PM
  • Saturday: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM

The online system is available 24/7 for book searches, pre-booking, renewals, and fine payments.
